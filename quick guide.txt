3. FEATURES TO TEST
-------------------------------------------------------

### A. DASHBOARD (Admin & Staff)
- View current shift details and quick statistics.
- Navigate quickly to the most important sections.

### B. THE SCHEDULE (Staff)
- Click 'Schedule' in the sidebar.
- View your upcoming shifts in a calendar format. 
- You should see the pre-seeded shifts for today and tomorrow.

### C. SHIFT SWAPS (Staff & Admin)
1. Login as STAFF (staff@fosms.com).
2. Go to 'Swaps' page.
3. Submit a 'Request Swap' by choosing a date and a colleague (e.g., Jane Smith).
4. Logout and Login as ADMIN (admin@fosms.com).
5. Go to 'Swaps' - you will see the incoming request.
6. Approve or Reject the request.

### D. USER MANAGEMENT (Admin Only)
1. Login as ADMIN.
2. Go to 'Manage Users'.
3. Click 'Add New User' to provision a new account.
4. Try searching for existing users using the search bar.

### E. OPERATIONAL REPORTS (Admin Only)
1. Login as ADMIN.
2. Go to 'Reports'.
3. View the 'Shift Distribution' bar chart.
4. Click 'Export to CSV' to download a data report for external analysis.

-------------------------------------------------------
